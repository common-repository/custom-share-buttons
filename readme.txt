Add custom share buttons to all of your posts,pages and/or categories.

== Description ==

The Custom Share Buttons does exactly what it says on the tin – adds share buttons to all of your posts and pages, simply.

You can even upload and use your own custom images if you wish!

The Custom Share Buttons provide the options for show/hide the share buttons on home,pages, posts and category.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the 'custom-share-buttons' folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Navigate to the csbtns Admin Panel via ‘Plugins’ -> ‘Share Buttons’ to change the settings as desired.
4. This is the csbtns Admin menu, where you can change the appearance and placement of your Share Buttons.
5. Upload and use your own custom images.
6. Drag, drop and reorder the buttons you wish to include.
7. Show your buttons using [csbtns] or hide them entirely from a page using [csbtns_hide].

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

1.How add custom share buttons on my page?

You have just need to add [csbtns] sortcode where you want add this.

2.Can i hide custom share buttons from any specific page?

Yes, you have just need to add [csbtns_hide] sortcode in your page


3.Is there any options in admin for show/hide the buttons on blog home page?

Yes, setting is avilable for that in admin under "General Setting" (>>Custom Share Buttons)
